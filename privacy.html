Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026 — Subject to attorney review prior to payment activation
SilqueFans is designed with privacy as a core value. We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the platform. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers.

1. Information we collect

When you create an account we collect your email address, username, and password (stored as a secure hash). When you complete your profile you may optionally provide a display name, bio, and social links. We collect transaction data when payments are processed. We collect standard server logs including IP addresses and browser information.

2. How we use your information

  • To operate and maintain your account
  • To process payments and distribute creator earnings
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, payment receipts)
  • To enforce our content rules and investigate violations
  • To improve the platform

3. What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use your content to train AI models
  • We do not share creator identities with subscribers without consent
  • We do not retain payment card data — all payment processing is handled by our payment processors

4. Age verification

Age verification for creators accessing Tier 3 and Tier 4 content features is handled by third-party KYC providers. These providers collect and retain identity documents. SilqueFans receives only a verified age confirmation token — we do not store copies of identity documents on our servers.

5. Data sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the platform: our hosting provider (Netlify), our database provider (Supabase), our payment processors, and our email service provider. These providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

6. Data retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account we delete your personal data within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements. Transaction records may be retained longer as required by law.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, and export your data. To exercise these rights contact us at privacy@silquefans.com.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns — this data is anonymized.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure password hashing, and row-level database security. No security system is perfect — if you discover a security vulnerability please contact us at security@silquefans.com.

10. Children

SilqueFans is strictly for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we discover an account belongs to a minor it will be immediately terminated.

11. Changes

We will notify users of material changes to this policy via email before they take effect.

12. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@silquefans.com
